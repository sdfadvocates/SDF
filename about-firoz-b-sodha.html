About Advocate Firoz B. Sodha

Firoz Sodha (also known as Advocate Firoz B. Sodha) is the Managing Partner and Founder of SDF Associates, one of the most respected law firms in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. With 25+ years of legal experience, Firoz Sodha has established himself as a distinguished advocate known for his sharp legal acumen, unwavering commitment to justice, and a client-first approach.

Firoz Sodha's extensive practice spans Criminal Law, Corporate Law, Family Law, Civil Litigation, and Real Estate Law. He has successfully represented hundreds of clients before the Gujarat High Court, District Courts, and various tribunals, earning a reputation for thorough case preparation and persuasive courtroom advocacy.

Biography & Career Journey

Born and raised in Gujarat, Advocate Firoz B. Sodha developed an early passion for law and justice. After completing his legal education from Gujarat University, he embarked on his legal career with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality legal representation to individuals and businesses alike.

In the early years of his practice, Firoz B. Sodha honed his skills in criminal defense and civil litigation, handling a diverse range of cases that shaped his comprehensive understanding of Indian law. His dedication and courtroom success led to the founding of SDF Associates, which has since grown into a full-service law firm with a team of 12+ expert lawyers.

Over two decades, Advocate Firoz B. Sodha has handled 500+ cases across multiple practice areas, consistently delivering favorable outcomes for his clients. His ability to navigate complex legal challenges with strategic precision has made him one of the most sought-after advocates in Ahmedabad.
