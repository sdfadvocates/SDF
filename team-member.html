About

With a keen eye for detail and a passion for corporate governance, our associate brings a fresh perspective to business law. Graduated with honors from a leading law university, they have since built a robust practice advising startups, SMEs, and established businesses on legal compliance and strategic growth.

Their practice spans business formation, contract drafting and negotiation, regulatory compliance, and mergers & acquisitions advisory. Known for pragmatic counsel that balances legal rigor with business objectives, they help clients navigate complex commercial landscapes with confidence.

Beyond litigation, they are actively involved in legal education initiatives and frequently contribute to seminars on emerging trends in corporate law and business regulation. Mo Jilani works closely under the guidance of Advocate Firoz B. Sodha, the Managing Partner of SDF Associates.
